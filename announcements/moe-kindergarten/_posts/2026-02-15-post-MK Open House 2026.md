If your child will be entering K1 in 2027, we warmly invite you to join us at our Open House on 7 March, 8.00am – 12.30pm.

Come and discover where your child’s learning journey begins.

During the Open House, you can:
* Explore our curriculum discovery hubs
* Experience children-led tours
* Visit our engaging indoor and outdoor learning spaces
* Attend the Centre Head Insights Session
* Connect with our educators at our Tea &amp; Talk Corner

At MK@Yishun, we believe in giving every child a strong and confident start to their early years.

A strong start you can count on.

To register, please scan the QR code below or click <a href="https://mkyishun-openhouse2026.app.entry.gov.sg/form/eec5e5db-60ed-4fad-8f04-e5f3f52948bb" target="_blank">here</a>. 

We look forward to welcoming you and your family.
