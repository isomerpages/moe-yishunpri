# 2018

National Stamp Collecting Competition 
--------------------------------------

<p style="text-align: justify;">Yishun Primary School participated in the National Stamp Collecting Competition 2018 organised by Singapore Philatelic Museum. The annual competition seeks to promote the study of postage stamps and philatelic materials to learn about the world. Participants selected and researched on a topic of their choice, and presented their findings through the English Language and a variety of philatelic materials. The competition enabled participants to sharpen their research and organisation skills as well as their language and writing skills. Kudos to these pupils and the teachers who had prepared them for this competition.</p>

 We attained 3 Silver Awards and 1 Bronze Award.

MOE Innergy (School) Awards 2018
--------------------------------

<p style="text-align: justify;">The Innergy Award aims to recognise teams that have spent time developing, testing and implementing innovations to bring about significant benefits in MOE. All innovations are ground-up innovative projects and have been successfully implemented to significantly add value to teaching or work processes.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">Schools can nominate individuals or teams for the Innergy (Schools) Awards 2018, for innovative projects to enhance Teaching and Learning (T&L) or Work Processes (WP) implemented between 01 Jan 2017 and 31 Dec 2017.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify;">The 3 projects from Yishun Primary School have achieved excellent outcomes.</p>

![](/images/Achievements/2018/Innergy2018_1.jpg)

**Project S•C•R•E•E•N (WP) – Gold Award**

<p style="text-align: justify;">Project S•C•R•E•E•N is the acronym for <b>S</b>hare, <b>C</b>ollaborate, <b>R</b>eflect & <b>E</b>ngage via <b>E</b>dmodo• <b>N</b>etwork. This school innovation was used to engage pupils and their parents during the Overseas Immersion Programmes to Shanghai and Bangkok in 2017 and also Brunei in 2018.</p>

![](/images/Achievements/2018/Innergy2018_2.jpg)

**3S Programmes@YPS (T&L) – Commendation Award**

The Chinese Language Unit designed the 3S Programmes@YPS which provides a holistic approach to develop the students into active reader (_STAR_ Programme), confident speakers (_SHINE_ Programme) and passionate writers (_SHARE_ Programme).

![](/images/Achievements/2018/Innergy2018_3.jpg)

**Building on our Knowledge: Our Lego Programme (T&L) - Commendation Award**

The Tamil Language Unit designed the “Building on our Knowledge - LEGO programme”. The innovation aims to engage and motivate the students to speak the Tamil Language confidently and also to enhance their all-round communication skills
